Akshairaj T

Founder, Lume

I'm a telecom engineer and watch enthusiast from India. After years of collecting watches, I realized there wasn't a single place where collectors could truly manage and enjoy their hobby. Collection details lived in spreadsheets, service records were scattered across emails, reminders were easy to forget, and sharing wrist checks or connecting with other collectors happened on completely different platforms.

So I built Lume — a modern companion designed specifically for watch collectors. Whether you're tracking your first watch or managing a growing collection, Lume helps you organize your collection, preserve every watch's story, and share your journey with fellow enthusiasts.

Lume is built on a simple belief: watch collecting is more than owning watches — it's preserving stories, chasing goals, and being part of a community that understands the journey.

Built by a collector, for collectors.
