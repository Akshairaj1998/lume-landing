Delete Account

You can delete your Lume account and all associated data directly from within the app.

Step 1

Open the Lume App

Sign in to your account and navigate to the Profile tab using the bottom navigation bar.

Step 2

Go to Settings

Scroll down to the bottom of your profile and tap Privacy Settings, or tap the settings icon.

Step 3

Delete Your Account

Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account. Confirm the action when prompted.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, photo, bio)
  • Your entire watch collection and service records
  • All wrist check posts and reminders
  • Your dream watch goal and savings data
  • Your account credentials and authentication data
Delete Specific Data

Remove Individual Items Without Deleting Your Account

You can delete specific data at any time from within the app:

  • Watches: Open the watch → tap the options menu → Delete Watch
  • Wrist Check Posts: Open the post → tap the options menu → Delete Post
  • Service Records: Open the service record → tap Delete
  • Reminders: Swipe left on a reminder → tap Delete
  • Profile Photo: Go to Profile → Edit → remove photo

Deleted items are removed from our servers immediately and cannot be recovered.

Need Help?

Contact Support

If you are unable to access the app and need your account deleted, email us at support@lumeapp.io with the subject line "Delete Account Request". We will process your request within 7 days.
